Welcome to Pailin (Cambodia Ruby/RubyOnRails User Group)

Cambodia Ruby/RubyOnRails User Group. Pailin is a place for Rubyists in Cambodia. People who are writing Ruby programming codes and/or developing RubyOnRails web applications, here is a place for us to share common view on this technology as well as sharing your Software Engineering experiences.
